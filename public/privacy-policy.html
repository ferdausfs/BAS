Privacy Policy

Bake Art Style — cake ordering app

1. Who we are

Bake Art Style ("BAS", "we", "us", "our") is a homemade cake business based in Comilla, Bangladesh. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect through our ordering app (the "App"), how we use it, and the choices you have.

By creating an account or placing an order through the App, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy.

2. Information we collect

  • Account information: your name, phone number, and email address, provided directly by you or received from a sign-in provider (Google or Facebook).
  • Facebook Login information: when you choose "Continue with Facebook," we receive your public profile (name, profile picture) and email address from Facebook, so we can create and identify your account. We do not post to Facebook on your behalf, and we do not share your information with Facebook for advertising purposes.
  • Delivery information: delivery address, district, and — if you choose to share it — GPS location, so we can deliver your order accurately.
  • Order information: items ordered, order history, delivery preferences, gift messages, wishlist, and reviews/photos you choose to submit.
  • Payment verification: when you pay via bKash or Nagad, you may upload a screenshot of your payment as proof. We use this only to verify and process your order. We do not collect or store card numbers, bank details, or mobile-wallet PINs.
  • Technical information: basic device and browser information, and locally stored app data (such as your cart and preferences), used to make the App work correctly.

3. How we use your information

  • To create and manage your account
  • To process, prepare, and deliver your orders
  • To verify advance/remaining payments for your order
  • To communicate with you about your order (confirmation, delivery updates, support)
  • To operate loyalty points, referral rewards, and promotional offers
  • To improve the App and troubleshoot problems

4. Where your information is stored

Your account and order data is stored using Google Firebase (Firestore and Cloud Storage). Images you upload (such as payment screenshots or review photos) may also be stored via Cloudinary. These providers process data on our behalf under their own security and privacy standards.

5. Sharing of information

We do not sell your personal information. We share information only:

  • With our delivery staff, limited to what is needed to complete delivery (name, phone, address)
  • With our service providers (Firebase/Google, Cloudinary) strictly to operate the App
  • When required by law

The App is intended for general audiences placing food orders and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission or storage over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
